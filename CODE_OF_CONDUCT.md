# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors and maintainers to communicate directly, respectfully,
and in good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- focus feedback on code, design, and behavior
- assume good intent
- keep discussions specific and actionable
- avoid personal attacks, harassment, or hostility

## Scope

This applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, and other public
community spaces for `AgentInbox`.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, remove abusive content, or block
participants who repeatedly violate these expectations.
